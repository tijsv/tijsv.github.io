About me

Since I was 15 years old I'm in love with graphic design. First I started to teach myself the basics of photo manipulation and soon after I learned to design 2D and 3D logos. I pursued my passion and two years ago I graduated with honors from the AP university college of Antwerp as a crossmedia designer.

Last year I studied computer engineering at the university of Antwerp to level up my basic knowledge about programming. After that year I realised that I was ready to search for a job and so I did.

Now I'm a proud member of the development team at Think Tomorrow as a frontend developer and JavaScript ninja.
